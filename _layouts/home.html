How We Can Help

Nourishing Food Marketing helps you build strong consumer, customer, and company value propositions. We help you get clear about your Marketing Strategy and execute on it with our trusted network of freelancers & agencies.

Brand DNA

Developing strong brand equity is critical for your long-term success in a world where Private Label is constantly gaining share. Your brand must represent more than your products; it's about an experience and a lifestyle.

Mini Case Study: We ran a Brand DNA Workshop for Real Fancy Foods, creating their Mission, Values, and Beliefs. We then built on this work with a brand name exploration, resulting in three top names to investigate trademarking.

Product Positioning

70% of new CPG products fail. We help you get crystal clear about why consumers purchase your products so you beat the odds. What's the consumer need that your product fulfills? What do you do better than anyone else? Hint: it's probably not being gluten free.

Mini Case Study: We refined the product positioning of YES BAR to help them stand out in the crowded snack bar category by evaluating competitive products, researching market trends, and doing consumer research.

Consumer Insights

Hear from your consumers through scrappy quantitative and qualitative research. These actionable insights will help you make quick, informed decisions.

Mini Case Study: We developed a series of blind taste tests to inform Moonshot's product development. This included developing the consumer screener, writing the survey, running logistics, and analyzing the results.

Strategic Marketing Planning

Develop framework of business and marketing objectives and subsequent marketing workstreams, including marketing support plans for in-store distribution.

Mini Case Study: We created and executed a 2019 Marketing Support Plan for Petit Pot's new Walmart distribution. This integrated digital and shopper marketing campaign resulted in a 15% sales lift and expanded distribution to 1500 doors.
